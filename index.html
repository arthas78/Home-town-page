Visit Berdyansk

Enjoy the beautiful places of a small resort town on the shores of the Sea of Azov

About

Berdyansk is a port city in Zaporizhzhia Oblast south-eastern Ukraine.It serves as the administrative center of Berdiansk Raion. The city is named after the Berda Riverat the foot of which it is located.Its population is 106,311.Berdiansk is home to a safari zoo, water park, museums, health resorts with mud baths and climatic treatments, and numerous water sport activities. Berdiansk Airport is located nearby.

Top four activities to do in Berdyansk

Take a walk along the seaside square

There are many restaurants, cafes and entertainment, as well as the sandy shore of the Sea of Azov

Visit Aquapark "Mys Dobroyi Nadiyi"

Aqua park with a variety of water slides, swimming pools with loungers & a European restaurant

Distance "kosa" Beach

A nice place to have some rest if you prefer peace and wild nature to the noisy city with its crowded municipal beaches

Dolphinarium "Nemo"

It's an open air dolphinarium show, if you don't want to get wet its better not to sit in the front row
